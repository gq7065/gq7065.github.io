commom exercises

The following are common routines we encourge our clients to do in order to see results and achieve their fitness goals

Burpue
Burpues are a great way to increase you strength and endurance, begin a standing position, drop into a square and extend your hands forward.kick your feet back and forward again
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To perform a plank, get on a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor, hold the position as long as you can
Mountain climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise.Place your hands on a floor, on a push position. Bring one of your knees up to your chest, and switch as quick as you can
